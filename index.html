FROM THE HILLS OF KAVREPALANCHOK

We work directly with artisan families in the Kavrepalanchok district, where the Bodhichitta trees grow in abundance. Each seed is hand-selected, hand-drilled, and hand-strung with intention and care.

There are no factories here. No mass production. Only the patient hands of artisans who understand that these are not merely objects, but vessels of spiritual practice.
